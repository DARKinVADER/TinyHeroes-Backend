Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

⚠️

GDPR & COPPA Notice

This app stores information about children. By using TinyHeroes, you confirm you are the parent or legal guardian of any child whose data you add.

1. Introduction

TinyHeroes ("we", "us", "our") operates the TinyHeroes family good-deed tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. The data controller is TinyHeroes — contact us at privacy@tinyheroes.app.

2. Data We Collect

👤

Account

Email address, display name

🧒

Children

Names, ages, gender, emoji avatar or uploaded photo

⭐

Good Deeds

Deed descriptions, AI-generated deed illustrations

🔑

Authentication

JWT access token stored in browser localStorage (th_access_token)

👨‍👩‍👧

Family

Family name, week start preference, member roles

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate the app — to display podium standings, award prizes, and generate weekly and monthly summaries. Deed descriptions are sent to the Hugging Face Inference API to generate illustrations; no personal identifiers (names, photos) are included in those prompts.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored on our servers [location placeholder]. Uploaded child photos are stored as static files and served directly from our server. We do not use third-party CDNs, analytics, or tracking scripts.

5. Third Parties

We share data with the following third parties only:

🤖

Hugging Face

Deed descriptions only — for AI image generation. No personal data.

🔐

Google / Apple / Facebook (optional)

Only if you choose social sign-in. Governed by each provider's privacy policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or any other parties.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your family at any time via Settings → Family Settings → Danger zone. This permanently and irreversibly removes all child records, good deeds, photos, and history. For account deletion, contact us at privacy@tinyheroes.app.

7. Children's Privacy (GDPR & COPPA)

TinyHeroes is a tool for parents and guardians. It may store data about children under 13. We rely on the account-holding parent or guardian to provide verifiable consent on behalf of their children. If you believe data about a child has been added without consent, contact us immediately and we will remove it.

✉️

Questions about your privacy?

Reach us at privacy@tinyheroes.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
